Applicants should note that the Washington DC center shall only open at 9 AM. Applicants will not be allowed inside the building or in the building lobby prior to 9 AM. Applicants are requested not to arrive at our application center prior to 9 AM as this is causing a lot of inconvenience to other tenants in the building.

Information for Applicants with Disabilities

  • The entrance is clear and unobstructed.
  • A friend / relative can accompany customers with disabilities throughout the application process.
  • There are clear, easy to read signs in English.
  • The building is very well lit inside.

Parking Facility

There is metered parking available outside the application centre. Parking outside the application centre is at the owner's risk. A parking garage is located behind our building on 24th Street, the cost for parking is $17.00.
