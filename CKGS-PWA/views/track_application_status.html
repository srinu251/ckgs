Important Note on FedEx shipping through CKGS:

DO’S
  • Please go to a FedEx Office location to send documents. Log onto - www.fedex.com/locate/index.html?#start
    to locate your nearest FedEx location.
  • At FedEx, only use the packaging to send you document
  • Weight of the package should be less than 0.5Lbs.
  • Only 1 Application per package.
DONT's
  • DO NOT request a pickup by FedEx.
  • DO NOT ship using any other packaging.
  • If more than one applications are sent, then the application will be on hold till the balance reimbursable payments are paid.
  • If you have used your own prepaid labels, we cannot take any responsibility of pick-up and delivery to you. The Shipping Service Providers often do not acknowledge receipt of the envelopes. Nor will CKGS drop off your prepaid envelopes to your shipping service provider as you have not bought the shipping service from us. 
  • FedEx Prepaid Envelopes sent by you are NOT accepted.
